THE FRAMEWORK

Five Letters.
Infinite Possibilities.

F
Format
Define the output structure. Bullet points, tables, paragraphs — tell AI exactly how to respond.
I
Identity
Assign expertise. "You are a senior data scientist" changes everything AI produces.
R
Reference
Provide context. Examples, documents, data — the more specific, the better the output.
S
Specifics
Detail the requirements. Word count, tone, audience, constraints — leave nothing to assumption.
T
Tone
Set the voice. Professional, casual, empathetic, authoritative — match your brand.
